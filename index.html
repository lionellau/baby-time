How to Play

🍼
Live the little moments. Your child grows from age 1 to 6. Each day brings a real-life moment — a tantrum, a bedtime, a first step. Choose how to respond.
🌈
Watch them grow. Your responses nurture ❤️ Bond, 💬 Language, 🤸 Motor skills and 🌈 Feelings (emotional skills). There are no fatal mistakes — children are resilient, and repair always matters more than perfection.
⚡
Mind your own battery. Engaged parenting takes energy. When yours runs low, everything is harder — the game gently reminds you that caring for yourself is caring for your child.
🎲
Every child is different. Each new game rolls a temperament — sunny, spirited, cautious, or sensitive. The same response can land differently with a different child. That's parenting!
🧸
Play together. Each year includes a little play session — peekaboo, block towers, feeling faces — because play is how children learn.
📚
Learn as you go. Every choice comes with a tip drawn from widely-shared early-childhood guidance (responsive caregiving, emotion coaching, reading together). It's a game, not a manual — real children write their own rules.
⌨️
